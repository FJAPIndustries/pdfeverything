Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 January 2026

PDFEverything ("the App") is a PDF editing application designed to allow users to view, edit, annotate, and manage PDF documents. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

PDFEverything does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers. All document processing occurs locally on your device unless explicitly stated otherwise.

a. Files and Documents

The App requires access to files on your device in order to:

  • Open and display PDF documents
  • Edit, annotate, or modify PDF files
  • Save edited PDFs back to your device

Your files remain on your device and are not uploaded, shared, or analyzed by us.

b. Device Permissions

PDFEverything may request the following permissions, which are standard for PDF editing applications:

  • Storage / Files access – to read and save PDF documents
  • Media access – to import images into PDFs (if supported)
  • Camera access – to scan documents or capture images for PDF creation (if enabled)

These permissions are used solely to provide core app functionality.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

  • Collect personal identifiers (such as name, email address, or phone number)
  • Track user location
  • Monitor user activity outside the App
  • Sell or share data with third parties

3. Third-Party Services

PDFEverything does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Data Security

Because PDFEverything processes files locally on your device, you retain full control over your data. We recommend protecting your device with appropriate security measures such as a lock screen and keeping your operating system up to date.

5. Children’s Privacy

PDFEverything does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is intended for general productivity use.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact the developer via the PDFEverything GitHub repository.
